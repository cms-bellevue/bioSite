Big Time Bill Collier

Bill Collier is a professional wrestler known across the northeast for his unmatched work ethic, powerful presence, and technical skill. With a career that began in 2007, he quickly established himself as a force to be reckoned with, earning multiple championship titles and making a name for himself in various promotions.

Collier's dedication extends beyond the ring; he balances his rigorous training and travel schedule with a full-time job as a heavy machinery operator and his commitment to his family as a husband and father. His story is a testament to perseverance, grit, and the pursuit of a dream against all odds.

Career Highlights

A native of Johnstown, PA, Bill Collier started wrestling in 2007 after training with Ken “Kato” Downs. He rose quickly on the independent scene, winning championships across the Northeast. Collier’s matches on AEW Dark: Elevation against stars like Jon Moxley and Hangman Page showcased his talent on a national level.

Beyond the spotlight, Bill’s a full-time heavy machinery operator, husband, and father. His story is built on perseverance and grit—balancing blue-collar life with the world of pro wrestling. Whether in IWC, ESW, or TNA as William Trudo, he brings a veteran presence and unmatched work ethic.
