Big Time Bill Collier
Home About Behind the Scenes

Bill Collier is a professional wrestler known across the northeast for his unmatched work ethic, powerful presence, and technical skill. With a career that began in 2007, Bill has gone on to hold numerous titles across regional and national promotions. Known as "Big Time," he's faced some of the biggest names in wrestling, bringing the fight to every show he steps into. Outside the ring, he's a mentor, a working man, and a representation of what it means to grind for greatness. Whether it’s through his in-ring storytelling or his genuine connection to fans, Bill represents the blue-collar warrior spirit—someone who never quits, no matter how tough the match.

Stats

  • Height: 6'5"
  • Weight: 265 lbs
  • Debut: 2007
  • Titles Held: ESW Champion, IWC Super Indy Champion, Greektown Tag Champion

Career Highlights

A native of Johnstown, PA, Bill Collier started wrestling in 2007 after training with Ken “Kato” Downs. He rose quickly on the independent scene, winning championships across the Northeast. Collier’s matches on AEW Dark: Elevation against stars like Jon Moxley and Hangman Page showcased his talent on a national level.

Beyond the spotlight, Bill’s a full-time heavy machinery operator, husband, and father. His story is built on perseverance and grit—balancing blue-collar life with the world of pro wrestling. Whether in IWC, ESW, or TNA as William Trudo, he brings a veteran presence and unmatched work ethic.
