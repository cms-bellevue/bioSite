What time is it? BIG TIME!

"Big Time" Bill Collier isn't just a nickname; it's a statement about his character. Known for his explosive in-ring style, towering 6'5" frame, and relentless drive, Collier exemplifies what can be achieved when passion meets hard work. From local gyms to national promotions, he has earned respect at every career stage.
